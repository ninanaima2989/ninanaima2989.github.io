# About this Blog | حول هذه المدونة

## 🇬🇧 English

Welcome to my **automated daily blog** focused on three exciting tech domains:

- 🤖 **Artificial Intelligence** — LLMs, computer vision, generative AI
- 🔒 **Cybersecurity** — Zero-trust, threat intel, defensive techniques
- 📊 **Data Science** — MLOps, analytics, big data architectures

Every day at **12:00 UTC**, a new bilingual article is automatically generated and published using Google Gemini AI and GitHub Actions.
